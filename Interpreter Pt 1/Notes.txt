Mstate is a function that takes the big list given by the parser and recursively determines where each sublist should go (ie what function should be called).

Sub-functions:
M_state_var
M_state_=
M_state_if
M_state_while
M_state_return

M_bool_==
M_bool_!=
M_bool_>
M_bool_<
M_bool_>=
M_bool_<=

M_value_+
M_value_-
M_value_*
M_value_/
M_value_%
